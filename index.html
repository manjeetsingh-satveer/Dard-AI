Dard Flare Risk Score (DFRS) — Proprietary Algorithm
DFRS = 10 × ( Σ (wᵢ · xᵢ) / Σwᵢ )

Stress level (1–10)1.5×score / 10Segerstrom 2004: stress amplifies inflammatory response

Fatigue level (1–10)1.2×score / 10Secondary signal — validated in fibromyalgia scoring systems

Sleep Debt (Rolling 3-Day)
Savg = (n₁ + n₂ + n₃) / 3
Sdebt = max(0, 7 − Savg)
Srisk = Sdebt × 2.5
Cumulative sleep debt is more predictive than a single bad night. Rolling average smooths outliers.
Pressure Risk Contribution
Prisk = max(0, (1013 − hPa) / 10)
Prisk = min(Prisk, 3.0)
990 hPa storm → contributes +2.3
Barometric drops below 1010 hPa are clinically significant. Capped at 3.0 to prevent dominance.
Condition Match Confidence
raw = matched_markers / total
penalty = (1 − test_coverage) × 0.4
conf = raw × (1 − penalty)
Inspired by Framingham Risk Score. Penalises confidence when key tests haven't been done.
